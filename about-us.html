About Us

The Coalition for University Equity is a student lead coalition and non-profit that was formed in 2024 with a goal of helping minority groups at Iowa State University. The main goal of CUE is to spread awareness of harmful legislation impacting minority groups on campus. CUE is also invested in sharing information on how to help and where impacted individuals can find community and safety.

Our Values

CUE's core values are as follows:

We believe in upholding Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion, regardless of what Iowa State University does to try to prevent said values from being shared in classrooms or by other forms of formal communication. We also believe that free speech should be upheld and that we have the right to share our thoughts on current events impacting minority groups on campus.
